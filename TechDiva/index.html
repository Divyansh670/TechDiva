Debjani Ghosh

Debjani Ghosh: President of NASSCOM, driving Indian IT growth, advocate for diversity, and tech evangelist empowering women in technology.

Padmasree Warrior

Padmasree Warrior: Former Cisco CTO, tech executive, and entrepreneur, driving innovation in autonomous vehicles, cybersecurity, and advocating for gender diversity in tech leadership.

Neha Narkhede

Neha Narkhede: Co-founder of Confluent, Apache Kafka co-creator, leading innovations in real-time data streaming, and empowering data-driven enterprises globally.

Ruchi Sanghvi

Ruchi Sanghvi: Facebook's first female engineer, entrepreneur, and venture capitalist, contributing to tech advancements and fostering innovation in Silicon Valley.

Aditi Kulkarni

Aditi Kulkarni: Principal Program Manager at Microsoft, cloud computing expert, driving digital transformation, and a strong advocate for diversity and inclusion in tech.

Shubha Nabar

Shubha Nabar: Senior Director of Data Science at Salesforce, machine learning specialist, advancing AI applications, and promoting diversity in the tech industry.
